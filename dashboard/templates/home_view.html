Welcome to the SpongeBob fan page

This is a fan page dedicated to telling you all about the Nickelodeon television program SpongeBob SquarePants

SpongeBob SquarePants is a famous T.V. series that airs on Nickelodeon. It has ran since 1999 with Help Wanted and is still airing today. It has a total of 248 ordered episodes with 180 aired. It is Nick's longest series surpassing Rugrats. The series shows the adventures of Spongebob Squarepants, Patrick Star, Sandy Cheeks, Squidward Tentacles, Eugene Krabs, Plankton, Gary, and lots of other fish living under the sea. It takes place in Bikini Bottom, a town which population has changed over the year with different citizens moving in and out because of different events including global warming, destruction, or even some push factors.
